Our theme

Empower Fields, Ignite Miles

"Empower Fields, Ignite Miles" is not just a tagline, it's a powerful call to action that embodies our commitment to supporting farmers and raising awareness about their challenges. In this marathon, we come together as a community to run not just for ourselves, but for those who tirelessly cultivate our fields. By participating, we lend our collective energy to amplify the voices of farmers, igniting a movement that sheds light on the issues they face. As we log miles, we symbolically bridge the gap between urban and rural, standing in solidarity with those who feed the world. Join us in this transformative journey as we empower fields with our steps and ignite miles of change, all while supporting and advocating for our farmers.

3 Km - Fun Run

Route

3 KM Run will commence from the COEP Hostel ground ,taking an immediate left after leaving main gate ,the marathon will proceed to JM road ,taking right turn from MODERN sqaure and making its way toward FC road. continuing with FC road MARATHON will proceed to POLICE HEADQUARTER . Then taking right turn making its way back to Sancheti Hospital , Taking Right and entering back to COEP Ground thorugh the Finishing Gate.

5 Km - Fitness / Corporate

Route

This Race will as well commence from the COEP Hostel Ground, following onto JM Road taking right at JM Corner, and heading towards the Shimla Office. Take the left from there and continue straight on University Road for approximately 2.1 KM. At the intersection before Cosmos Bank and after ABIL House, take a U-turn and follow straight back to the Sancheti Hospital. After taking a right and entering the ground through Finishing Gate will conclude the race.
Registration Fees - ₹ 749/- (Offer - ₹ 649/-)

10 Km

Route

This Race will follow a similar pattern for all other categories, COEP Hostel Ground-JM Corner-Shimla Office then to University Road. Continue straight on University Road for about 2.63 KM, reaching University Circle. Continued to a left on Pashan Road, then at an intersection of Pashan Road and Abhimanashree Society Road take right and follow straight till Baner Road is reached. After reaching Baner Road take an immediate left and take the first U-turn on that road (will be guided by the volunteers of the fest), then continue straight to the University Circle on Baner Road, followed by Cosmos Bank, ABIL House then back to the Sancheti Hospital taking right and entering back to the ground through Finishing Gate.
Registration Fees - ₹ 1049/- (Offer - ₹ 899/-)
